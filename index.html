Provenance tells you
who made something.
It does not tell you
who agreed to it.

Every piece of content now carries metadata about where it came from. That is the C2PA standard. It is necessary and good. But it only answers one question: who created this?

It does not answer the question that matters most: did the person depicted in this content consent to this specific use, right now, today?

DDT answers that question. In real time. With a revocable, machine-readable record that travels with the content and never lies.

The Five Permission States

Authorized Consent is active and verified
Revoked Consent withdrawn by the creator
Suspended Use paused pending review
Flagged Requires human review
Out of Scope This use was never permitted
