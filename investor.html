The Structural Moat

Why DDT is defensible.

01
Live revocability changes everything

Static consent records are not consent. DDT's Identity Permission State is modeled on OCSP: always live, always current, always fail-closed. No static document can compete.

02
The subject layer is genuinely new

C2PA covers creator provenance. No standard covers subject consent. DDT's proposed org.c2pa.digital-likeness assertion fills the gap no one else has addressed in standards.

03
Union distribution creates network density

SAG-AFTRA, WGA, Equity UK: each union that distributes CRO infrastructure to members creates instant density platforms cannot ignore.
